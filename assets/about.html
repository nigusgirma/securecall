SecureCall
	The app SecureCall gives encrypted voice and SMS end-to-end and are impossible to listen in to from outside hackers. It is also safeguarded for Man in the middle attack. The app is an IP mobile app and can run on wifi if Internet access is available, 3G and 4G networks. If wifi and Internet are available it prioryties those networks which may give the user cost reductions on their mobile phone budget. You can look at SecureCall as an IP phone but with encryption end-to-end for voice and SMS included. If you donnot have access to wifi/Internet it uses the 3G or 4G networks, putting a secure protocol on top of these networks, in this way the app works without direct wifi/Internet access as well.
